Playdate Recommendations

If you are new to Playdate or just want some game recommendations here is a list of my top Playdate games (as of 3/6/2026). These are the ones I enjoyed the most, I try to buy and play other Playdate games when I am not actively developing anything. I am not including anything I was involved with or anything from Season 1 since you get all those games automatically with the device. There are some great games in the first season. Anyway, here is the list:

Tiny Turnip - Tiny Turnip is a climbing adventure that beautifully integrates all of the Playdate's controls...

Devils On The Moon Pinball - Devils On The Moon Pinball is a beautifully, meticulously crafted masterpiece...

Root Bear - Root Bear is a Playdate staple that challenges you to create the perfect pour...

Fulcrum Defender - Fulcrum Defender is a simple defense-style shooter...

Under The Castle - Under The Castle is my favorite turn-based RPG on the Playdate...

Dig! Dig! Dino! - Dig! Dig! Dino! tasks players with uncovering dinosaur bones...

Icy Dungeon - Icy Dungeon features 70 carefully designed levels...

Water Flow - Water Flow is a deceptively simple puzzle game...
